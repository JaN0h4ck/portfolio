About the game

The world as we once knew it no longer exists and humanity is only, like the dinosaurs, an extinct fossil. But who inhabits it now? Quite simply, the breads!
 

The majority of the population relies on the holy sourdough starter, which is strictly guarded by the Breadtican. However, one day this starter is mysteriously stolen! So now it’s up to all units of the Dough Patrol to urgently find it again, even for those who may have let themselves go a bit over time. Experience in Republic Bread together with the Brotagonist Benjamin Brötchen, a detective who has been left standing for a long time, the story that's been cookin! Solve crumbly puzzles and look for crumbs that can show you the way to catch the perpetrator. “Republic Bread” was created as part of the GE-Lab project work in the degree "Informatics - Game Engineering" in the winter semester 2022/23 by the students Jan Nothacker, Alysha Oberhofer-Biyikli, Lukas Pusch, Maximilian Löns, Ray Schauer and Bianca Rizzoli. The students were supervised by Prof. Dr. René Bühling during development.
